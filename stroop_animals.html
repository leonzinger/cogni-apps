Stroop Colors Effect Assessment test

In Stroop “Animals” you will see animals. At first, the head, body and name of the animal will match.

Stroop Effects will move from animal to animal and ask you which animal you see. Select from the menu at the bottom of the screen the correct answer.

In Stage 2, the animal head and body will still match, but the name of the animal has been removed.Select the correct animal from the options menu.

In the next three stages of Stroop, the bodies and heads of the animals will NOT match.

Your job is to name the animal represented by theBODY of each animal illustration.

If you see a parrot’s head on a dog’s body, for instance, select “dog” from the menu, if on mouse’ body, select “mouse”, on the lion’s body, select “lion”.

The objective is to ignore the “parrot” name tag and the parrot’s head, and choose the animal whose bodies presented.
